My Buggy Working Day

[2013]

This little poem was inspired by my first job in outsourcing. Those days are so far away...
Many great thanks to Sviatlana B., the English teacher who helped me to polish the poem!

I arrived at work at nine,
I was feeling really fine.
I updated, started build
And came back to LinuxGeek.

Half an hour wasted. "Blame!
Let's return to work again!"
Then I read in my console:
"Oh my God, the build is broken!"

"Make it working? No, not now.
I have the book for the next hour.
I will read and will grow up!"
But the teamlead gave me bug.

"Bug's not critical... Okay.
Will be fixed." - as I would say.
Then I imitated my work.
And left my bug untouched at all.

I again surfed the Inet
And again I searched the Web
"How to do?" and "how to be?"
But my bug was still on me.

Nothing done and nothing changed,
It did seem a little strange.
Oh, I wasted lots of time.
The teamleader asked me now:

"What was done and will be done?"
"How to answer?.. All is fine!
I'm in progress" - then I said.
- "Good, then finish it today."

Kitchen noise attracted me
And I went away from here...
I returned to my workplace,
Hour wasted. "What's a mess?!!"

"One and one - it will be two,
Half an hour plussing too,
One for reading, one for tea,
But my bug is still on me."

"What do to and how to do?
How to be in my job good?
How to deal with painful bugs?
Maybe ask my team at once?

Maybe they will take my pain?
And for all it will be gain.
I keep silent, I don't know
How to say and ask at all.
How to say "Let's do my work.
Help me, please! I'm in deadlock!!!"

Day was over. I went home.
Hope tomorrow it is done.
